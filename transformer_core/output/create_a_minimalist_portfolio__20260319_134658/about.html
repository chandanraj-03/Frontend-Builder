About Alex Morgan

Bridging the gap between beautiful design and functional code for over 8 years.

My Philosophy

I believe exceptional digital experiences are born at the intersection of thoughtful design and clean, accessible code. My journey has taught me that the most successful projects happen when designers and developers speak the same language—and I've made it my mission to be fluent in both.

With a background in both visual arts and computer science, I approach every project with a unique dual perspective. I understand not just how a design should look, but how it should be built—considering performance, accessibility, and maintainability from the very first sketch.

My work is driven by a commitment to creating digital products that are not only beautiful but also inclusive, performant, and sustainable for the long term.
