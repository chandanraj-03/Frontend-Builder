Behind the Scenes

Video Production Insights

Director: Maya Chen
"For the Starlight Dreams video, we wanted to capture the feeling of urban isolation mixed with hope. We shot over three nights in downtown LA, using practical neon lights and minimal CGI."
Cinematographer: Alex Rivera
"We used vintage anamorphic lenses to create the dreamlike quality in the Midnight Drive video. The highway scenes were shot at 4am to get empty roads."
Lead Vocalist: Jordan
"The acoustic session video was special because it was completely live - no overdubs or fixes. It captured a raw, emotional performance that's different from our studio work."
