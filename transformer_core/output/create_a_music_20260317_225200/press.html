Official Biography

Aurora Wave

Aurora Wave is an electronic music duo formed in 2019 by vocalist Jordan Reed and producer Alex Chen. Based in Los Angeles, their sound blends atmospheric synth-pop with cinematic soundscapes, drawing comparisons to artists like CHVRCHES, M83, and Purity Ring.

Their debut EP "Electronic Dreams" (2022) gained critical acclaim for its innovative production and emotional depth. The follow-up album "Midnight Serenade" (2023) debuted at #12 on the Billboard Electronic Albums chart and has accumulated over 50 million streams worldwide.

The duo's 2024 World Tour spans three continents with sold-out shows across North America and Europe. Their live performances are noted for elaborate visual production and immersive lighting design.

Awards & Recognition:

  • 2023 Electronic Music Awards - Best New Artist (Nominee)
  • Billboard - "10 Electronic Artists to Watch in 2024"
  • Pitchfork - "Midnight Serenade: 7.8/10 - A compelling evolution in synth-pop"
  • NPR Music - "Featured Artist of the Week"
