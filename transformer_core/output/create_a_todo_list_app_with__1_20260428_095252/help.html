Frequently Asked Questions

How do I recover deleted tasks?

Deleted tasks can be restored within 30 days from the trash/recycle bin section in your settings. After 30 days, tasks are permanently removed.

Can I sync TodoMaster across multiple devices?

Yes! Enable cloud synchronization in Settings to access your tasks from any device with your account login.

How do I set up task reminders?

Reminders can be configured in task details or through global notification settings. You can set email, browser, or mobile notifications.

Is there a limit to the number of tasks I can create?

Free accounts can create up to 1000 tasks. Premium accounts have unlimited tasks and additional features.

How do I export my task data?

Navigate to Settings → Data Management → Export to download your tasks in JSON or CSV format for backup or analysis.
