About Mia

Mia Rodriguez is a Principal Security Architect with over 12 years of experience designing and implementing secure infrastructures for Fortune 500 companies and government agencies. She holds CISSP, CISM, and CCSP certifications and specializes in cloud security, Zero-Trust architectures, and threat intelligence.

Before joining Tech Insights, Mia led the security engineering team at a major financial institution where she architected their global Zero-Trust implementation, reducing security incidents by 93%. Her work has been featured in industry publications and she regularly speaks at conferences including Black Hat, RSA, and DEF CON.

Areas of Expertise

  • Zero-Trust Architecture Implementation
  • Cloud Security (AWS, Azure, GCP)
  • Identity and Access Management
  • Network Security & Micro-segmentation
  • Security Compliance (SOC 2, ISO 27001, NIST)
  • Incident Response & Threat Hunting

Professional Background

Mia began her career as a network security analyst at a regional bank before moving into consulting roles with major cybersecurity firms. She has helped organizations across finance, healthcare, and technology sectors build resilient security postures. Mia is passionate about mentoring the next generation of security professionals and volunteers with cybersecurity education nonprofits.

"Security shouldn't be an obstacle to innovation. The best security architectures enable business growth while protecting what matters most."
