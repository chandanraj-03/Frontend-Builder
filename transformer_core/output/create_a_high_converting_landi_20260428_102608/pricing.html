Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change plans later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll be charged the prorated difference for the remainder of your billing cycle. Downgrades take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

Do you offer annual billing discounts?

Yes! Annual billing saves you 20% compared to monthly payments. Choose annual billing during checkout to lock in this discount.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, and for Enterprise plans, we also accept bank transfers and purchase orders.

Is there a free trial?

Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial for all paid plans. No credit card required to start your trial. You'll have full access to all features during your trial period.

How does the money-back guarantee work?

If you're not satisfied with CloudFlow within the first 14 days of your paid subscription, contact our support team for a full refund. The guarantee applies to your first payment only.

Do you offer educational or nonprofit discounts?

Yes, we offer special pricing for educational institutions and registered nonprofits. Please contact our sales team with proof of status to learn about available discounts.
