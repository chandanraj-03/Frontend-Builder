Our Story

Learn about the passionate team behind CloudFlow and our mission to transform how businesses work through intelligent automation.

From Idea to Impact

CloudFlow was founded in 2018 by a team of former enterprise software engineers who were frustrated by the endless hours spent on repetitive tasks. We noticed that businesses were drowning in manual processes while their teams struggled to focus on strategic work.

What started as an internal tool to automate our own workflows quickly evolved into a comprehensive platform. After seeing 80% reductions in manual work and dramatic improvements in team productivity, we knew we had to share this solution with the world.

Today, CloudFlow serves over 10,000 companies worldwide, from startups to Fortune 500 enterprises. Our platform handles millions of automated workflows daily, saving our customers countless hours and enabling them to focus on innovation rather than administration.
