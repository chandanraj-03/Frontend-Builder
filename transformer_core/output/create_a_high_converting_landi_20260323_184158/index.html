Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a free trial?

Yes! All paid plans include a full-feature 14-day free trial. No credit card is required to start. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel anytime during the trial.

Can I change my plan later?

Absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from your account settings. Changes are prorated, and you'll only pay for the new plan on your next billing cycle.

How secure is my data?

Security is our top priority. We are SOC 2 Type II compliant, use enterprise-grade encryption (AES-256) for data at rest and in transit, and offer role-based access control. We never sell your data.

Do you offer onboarding support?

Yes. Pro and Enterprise plans include dedicated onboarding sessions. All plans have access to our comprehensive documentation, video tutorials, and responsive customer support team.
