Classic Chocolate Chip Cookies

Delicious, chewy, and perfect for any occasion. This recipe yields perfect cookies every time with just 4 simple ingredients.

⏰ Prep: 10 min | Cook: 12 min
🍴 4 servings
⭐ 4.7/5 rating

These classic chocolate chip cookies are the perfect combination of chewy center and crispy edges. The secret to their perfect texture is the precise balance of ingredients and baking technique.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1.5 cups white sugar
  • 0.5 cup brown sugar, packed
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1.5 cups chocolate chips

Step-by-Step Instructions

1

Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Line baking sheets with parchment paper.

2

In a large bowl, cream together butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until light and fluffy.

3

Beat in eggs one at a time, then add vanilla extract.

4

Stir in flour, baking soda, and chocolate chips until just combined.

5

Scoop rounded tablespoons of dough onto prepared baking sheets, spacing 2 inches apart.

6

Bake for 10-12 minutes or until edges are golden but centers are slightly underdone.

7

Let cookies cool on baking sheets for 5 minutes before transferring to wire racks.
